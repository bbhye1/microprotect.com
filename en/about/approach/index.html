Our Approach

Better insurance service for all

Microprotect redefines insurance.

Insurance started for those who are financially unable to afford it and that it should exist for that purpose.

But the current insurance is not.

Many insurances now seem to be far from the true value of insurance.

Most of all, because of the burdensome price, there are not many people who can keep even the insurance that they subscribed to.

It's even hard to understand true content of insurance.

Microprotect challenges this unfortunate reality.

We will combine our experience and knowledge of insurance with progressive technology to create new insurance that consumers can't see anywhere in the world.

Microprotect starts an insurance company with the following beliefs:

  1. We simply and clearly guarantee only what is essential to our customers.
  2. Providing easy and fast insurance service through innovative technology.
  3. Contribute to society as an essential insurance company.

Microprotect provides the insurance the world really needs.
